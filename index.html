Beautiful Scenery

Big Bear Lake is the place to enjoy the scenery. The lake itself looks astonishing and if you want to take it a step further (literally), there are mountain spots you can go to and enjoy the view of the forest, mountains and lake as well. Although, it does require a bit of exercise since you need to hike to the spots you want to go but trust me, it will be worth it.

Many Activities

There are many activities you can do in Big Bear Lake. Some examples like archery, where you can learn how to use a bow and arrow while doing target practice, or trying to hit the apple on top of the apple if you are up to the challenge. There is also ziplining, where you are strapped on to a cable and you ride through midair, feeling the excitement. It can be scary at first, but after many times, it is fun to ride.
